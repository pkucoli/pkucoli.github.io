NLPCC 2019: CALL FOR SHARED TASK PROPOSALS

The conference on Natural Language Processing and Chinese Computing (NLPCC) is the annual conference of CCF TCCI (Technical Committee of Chinese Information, China Computer Federation). The NLPCC conferences have been successfully held in Beijing (2012)ï¼ŒChongqing (2013), Shenzhen (2014), Nanchang (2015), Kunming (2016), Dalian (2017) and Hohhot (2018). This year's NLPCC conference will be held in Gansu.

The NLPCC 2019 cordially invites proposals for shared tasks at the conference. The topics of shared tasks include technology and application of natural language processing (NLP), e.g., core linguistic structure prediction, information extraction, sentiment analysis, question answering and dialogue system.

Proposals should contain the following information:

Names and affiliations of the members of the organizing committee (including a contact person)

  • A brief description of the shared task, including its relevance to the study of NLP

  • A brief description of the data set, including the process of preparing the data as well as the conditions of availability

  • A brief description of a plan on how the participating systems will be evaluated, including the availability of the evaluation software

  • Any other relevant information, including related evaluations for the proposed task

A tentative timeline for the NLPCC Shared Task 2019 is as follows:

  • February 15, 2019: announcements of shared tasks and call for participation

  • March 15, 2019: release of detailed task guidelines and training data

  • May 15 2019: release of test data

  • May 20 2019: the deadline for results submission

  • May 30 2019: release of evaluation results and call for system reports and conference papers

  • July 2019: conference paper submission deadline

  • July 2019: conference paper accept/reject decision meeting

  • October 12–14, 2019: NLPCC 2019 main conference

Proposals should make clear that a similar schedule can be implemented, especially for the preparation of resources.

The deadline to submit a proposal is February 10, 2019. We encourage proposers to include examples that help understand the data and type of evaluation. Proposals should be submitted by e-mail to ws@pku.edu.cn, from the email of the contact person, with email subject NLPCC-2019 Shared Task Proposal. Proposals will be reviewed by the NLPCC steering committee and proposers will be notified about the selection before February 14, 2019. Proposers might be contacted during the reviewing period to provide further information.

For any question related to this selection process, please email ws@pku.edu.cn or wechat wsun106.
